OVERVIEW

What is the Milky Way?

The Milky Way is a huge galaxy. A galaxy is a giant collection of stars, gas, dust and planetary systems held together by gravity.

Our Sun is one of the stars inside the Milky Way. The planets in our Solar System, including Earth and Mars, orbit the Sun.

Learning about the Milky Way helps us understand where we are in the universe and how planets can be very different from one another.

Quick space facts

  • The Milky Way contains billions of stars.
  • Earth is part of the Solar System.
  • Mars is often called the Red Planet.
  • Saturn is famous for its rings.
