PLANET FACTS

Interesting facts about planets

Every planet in our Solar System is different. Some are small and rocky, while others are large gas giants with many moons and rings.

Studying planets helps us understand how space works, how worlds are formed, and why Earth is such a special place for life.

Quick planet curiosities

  • Mercury is the closest planet to the Sun.
  • Venus is the hottest planet in the Solar System.
  • Earth is the only known planet with life.
  • Jupiter is the largest planet in the Solar System.
